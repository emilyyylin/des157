FUN FACTS

Here are some things that you might not know about sushi!


SASHIMI, NIGIRI.... WHAT?!

⊗
NIGIRI

Nigiri is raw fish over rice. TIP: Sometimes all you can eat sushi estaurants only offer nigiri because customers will be full off of eating chunks of rice (and they charge customers for leaving rice behind)! //Sneaky!!!

⊗
SASHIMI

Sashimi is just pure raw fish! People like to dip this in wasabi + soysauce to add that extra flavor, but apparently the reason people used wasabi in the first place is to "clean" the fish. You will get your money's worth if you just eat this at sushi buffets!

⊗
MAKI

Maki is the sushi roll with the seaweed outer layer. You can get creative by putting non-raw fish related food inside and use imitation crab, cucumber, carrot, avacado, eggs, etc. The most popular one is the California Roll!

⊗
URAMAKI

Uramaki is the sushi roll with a rice layer over the seaweed outer. Very similar concept to maki...just personal preference!

⊗
TEMAKI

Temaki is usually displayed upright in the tiny shelf or slots. It is basically raw fish or other contents wrapped with seaweed in a cone shape. These might be fragile, so be careful when holding it!
