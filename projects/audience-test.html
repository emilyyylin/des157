EZPZ

EZPZ is an app made mainly for people to be able to find cheap, easy, and healthy foods. It not only directs you to menu items in certain restaurants, but also directs you to which items in the near by market place that's convenient to purchase. On top of that, there is a platform for users to upload their own recipes and other users can create profiles and save/rate their favorite recipes.
